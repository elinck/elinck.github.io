A pro forma update from the south slope of Mt. Rainier, and the high desert of central Oregon. It's been a predictably busy end to the academic quarter, and the pineapple express is in town. You can either embrace bad conditions and hope they exceed expectations, or escape them. We've done both in the past three weeks. First turns of the 2014/2015 season, anyway.

But mostly, I've biked around in the rain and drunk coffee.
